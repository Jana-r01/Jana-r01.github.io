Some Fun Facts

Here is a list of interesting facts about reading and books in general to enrich one's knowlege and get him prepared to start reading:

  1. Finland is the world's most literate nation, according to new research.
  2. Top best selling book of all time: The DaVinci Code by Dan Brown
  3. World’s Highest-Paid Author: J.K. Rowling with 92 Million $.
  4. The world’s tallest book, “Collection of Maritime Rules”, is stored in the Amsterdam Museum. Its height is about 6.4 feet, and it’s over 3 feet wide.
  5. Three best-selling books in the world: the Holy Bible (6 billion copies), Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-Tung (900 million), and the Harry Potter series (500 million copies sold).
  6. The world’s most famous book thief is Steven Bloomberg who stole 23,000 rare books from 268 libraries. The total value of the stolen is estimated to be over $20 million! By the by, people who steal books are called bibliocleptomanes
  7. In the past, the value of the books was so high, that people had to chain books to the shelves. It was a highly effective theft prevention method and was widely practiced in the Middle Ages.

  • “Ex Libris: Confessions Of A Common Reader" by Anne Fadiman

  • “ Browsings: A Year Of Reading, Collecting, And Living With Books" by Michael Dirda

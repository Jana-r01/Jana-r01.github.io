The Book Industry

Contrary to popular belief, the book industry is not at risk of dying – far from it.
In fact, the industry has seen growth in the last few years, with book sales increasing in value by 1.4% in 2018 (according to Nielsen BookScan) – small growth, but growth nonetheless.

In this industry nowadays,consumers can find books anywhere from libraries to even websites
(where the industry is trying to incorporate itself trying to adapt to the world of today, so dependent on the Internet),
they can also purchase audible or visual forms of them, some of the most popular book selling websites are:

  • Internet Archive
  • Google Books
  • Audible (by amazon)

Associations and events for book worms

Most of the events related to the hobby of reading are tournaments and/or award ceremonies that are often held in schools and educational institutions, others can also be more known on an international level.
Some of the most popular are the HELBLING International Reading Competition that takes place in Itally annually and hosts all books worms and lovers and the The International Literacy Association (ILA) awards.
